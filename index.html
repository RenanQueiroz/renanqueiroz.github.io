About me

Hi, my name is Renan, and I am a junior high-school student. I am interested in anything tech-related, and I love to understand how things work. My favorite hobbies are gaming and customizing the software of my phones. In my free time, I usually work on Android development, and attempt to fix some of the problems on the builds I make.


In this website you will find all my recent works because it is being automatically updated all the time, to ensure that every new build I upload will appear here first. All the ROMs listed in this website will be frequently updated, being them LineageOS, Pixel Experience, and CypherOS. They will be updated at least once every month, so always remember to come back here to check if an update is out.


If you have any questions about my works, feel free to reach out to me using my socials available below, See you around!
